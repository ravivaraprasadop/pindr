✨ What is Pindr?

Pindr is a beautiful, minimalist to-do journal that lives in your Chrome browser. Create sticky boards for anything — tasks, quick notes, grocery lists, journaling, daily intentions — and have them always one click away.

Inspired by dotted bullet journals, Pindr brings the calm and clarity of analog journaling into your digital workflow.

🔐 Privacy Commitment

Pindr is built on a simple promise: your notes are yours. The extension has no backend server. The developer cannot see your boards, your email, or any data you create.

When you enable Google Drive sync, your data goes directly from your browser to your own Google account — Pindr is merely the messenger.

📬 Support & Feedback

Found a bug? Have a feature idea? You can reach the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing page. Your feedback directly shapes future versions of Pindr.
