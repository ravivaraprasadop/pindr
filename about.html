✨ What is Pindr?

Pindr is a beautiful, minimalist to-do journal that lives in your Chrome browser. Create sticky boards for tasks, quick notes, grocery lists, journaling, and daily intentions — always one click away from the toolbar.

Inspired by the calm clarity of dotted bullet journals, Pindr brings that same focused energy to your digital workflow — without the clutter of heavy productivity apps.

🔐 Privacy Promise

Pindr is built on one simple promise: your notes are yours. The extension has no backend server. The developer cannot see your boards, your email, or any data you create.

When you enable Google Drive sync, your data travels directly from your browser to your own Google account — Pindr is merely the messenger, never the recipient.
