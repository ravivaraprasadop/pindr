Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Pindr : To-Do Journal v2.1

🛡️ Short version: Pindr has no backend server. The developer cannot see any of your data. Your notes live on your device — or optionally in your own Google Drive.

📋 Overview

Pindr ("the Extension") is a personal to-do journal and sticky-note manager for Chrome. This policy explains what data the extension handles and how it is stored.

Pindr is designed with a privacy-first philosophy: no data ever leaves your own device or your own Google account.

💾 Data Stored Locally

All boards, notes, tasks, and settings are stored in your browser using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API. This includes:

  • Board titles and content (text, checkboxes, bullet points)
  • Board colours, pin state, and timestamps
  • Archived boards from previous days
  • Deleted boards temporarily in Trash (up to 7 days)
  • Your theme preference and display settings

This data is stored only on your device and is never sent to any server controlled by Pindr's developer.

☁️ Optional Google Drive Sync

Pindr offers an optional feature to back up your data to your own Google Drive. This feature:

  • Is entirely opt-in — you must explicitly sign in with Google to enable it
  • Saves a single file (pindr-data.json) directly to your Google Drive
  • Only requests the drive.file scope — Pindr can only access files it has created; it cannot read any other files in your Drive
  • Uses Google's official OAuth 2.0 via chrome.identity — no passwords seen or stored by Pindr

The developer cannot access your Google Drive, email, boards, or any synced data.

👤 Google Account Information

When you optionally sign in with Google, Pindr fetches basic profile info (name and photo) from Google's OAuth endpoint. This information is:

  • Stored only in chrome.storage.local on your device
  • Used solely to display your name and avatar in the extension header
  • Never sent to any third-party server
  • Immediately cleared when you sign out

🚫 What We Do NOT Do

  • ❌ We do not collect analytics or usage statistics
  • ❌ We do not serve advertisements
  • ❌ We do not share data with third parties
  • ❌ We do not have a backend server
  • ❌ We do not track your browsing activity
  • ❌ We do not read files outside of the pindr-data.json file you authorise

🔑 Permissions Explained

storage

Saves your boards and settings locally on your device.

unlimitedStorage

Ensures your boards are not automatically deleted by Chrome when disk space is low.

alarms

Powers the daily midnight board archiving and optional 30-minute auto-sync intervals.

notifications

Used only for task timer completion alerts when you set a timer on a task.

identity

Required for the optional Google Sign-In flow. Only contacted when you choose to sign in.

googleapis.com

Required for optional Google Drive sync. Only contacted when you are signed in and sync is active.

🗑️ Deleting Your Data

You can delete all data at any time:

  • In Pindr: Settings → Sync & Data → Clear all boards
  • In Chrome: chrome://extensions → Pindr → Details → Clear site data
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data automatically
  • If you used Drive sync: delete pindr-data.json from your Google Drive

📬 Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing page.
