Support & FAQ

Pindr : To-Do Journal · Version 2.1

💬 Most questions are answered below. If you still need help, reach out via the Chrome Web Store listing.
Getting Started
How do I create my first board? ▼
Click the "New Board" button in the top-right of the workspace. A new sticky board appears — click the title to rename it and start typing tasks or notes right away.
How do I pin a board to my popup? ▼
Click the 📌 pin icon in any board's header. Pinned boards appear instantly when you click the Pindr icon in your Chrome toolbar — no need to open the full workspace.
What's the difference between Checkbox and Bullet mode? ▼
Checkbox mode adds tick-able tasks — great for to-do lists. Bullet mode adds clean bullet points — great for notes and journaling. You can mix both freely in the same board using the toolbar buttons.
Can I reorder my boards? ▼
Pinned boards always appear at the top. Within sections, boards are ordered by creation date (newest first). Drag-to-reorder is on the roadmap for a future release.
Google Drive Sync
Is Google Drive sync required? ▼
Not at all. Pindr works perfectly without signing in. All boards are saved locally on your device. Drive sync is an optional backup — useful if you switch computers or want peace of mind.
What does Pindr access in my Google account? ▼
Only the drive.file scope — meaning Pindr can only see and edit the single file it created (pindr-data.json). It cannot read any other file in your Drive. It also reads your name and profile photo to display in the header.
Sync says "failed" — what do I do? ▼
Usually this means your Google session expired. Try signing out and signing back in. If it persists, check you have storage space in your Google Drive (free tier gives 15 GB).
How do I revoke Drive access? ▼
Click "Sign out" in the Pindr toolbar. To fully revoke access, visit myaccount.google.com/permissions and remove Pindr from the list.
Data & Privacy
Where is my data stored? ▼
All boards and settings are stored in chrome.storage.local on your device. The developer has no server and can see none of your data. If you enable Drive sync, a backup is saved in your personal Google Drive — not on any Pindr server.
I accidentally deleted a board. Can I recover it? ▼
Yes! Deleted boards go to the Trash. Boards stay in Trash for 7 days before being permanently removed. Open Settings and find the Trash section to restore any board.
How do I back up my data? ▼
Click "Export" in the toolbar. This saves a complete JSON backup of all your boards, archives, and settings. Re-import it anytime with the "Import" button.
What happens if I uninstall Pindr? ▼
Please export your data first! Uninstalling removes all locally stored data. If you had Drive sync on, your pindr-data.json will still be in your Google Drive — re-import it after reinstalling.
Features & Tips
What is Auto Archive? ▼
Every night at midnight, boards created on previous days are automatically moved to the Archive. Your workspace stays clean while your full history is preserved. Access it via the Archive button in the toolbar.
How do I change the theme? ▼
Click the 🌙 button in the top-right toolbar to cycle through Light, Dark, and Ambient (deep navy) themes. Or open Settings for a visual theme picker with previews.
How do I use the "To checks" button? ▼
Select some lines of text in a board, then click "↳ To checks" — those lines will be converted into checkbox rows. If nothing is selected, all plain text in the board is converted.
